# Google Ads — Performance Max & Responsive Display

## Performance Max Asset Requirements

PMax uses responsive assets that Google's AI combines and serves
across Search, Display, YouTube, Discover, Gmail, and Maps.

### Image Assets

| Asset Type | Dimensions | Ratio | Required? |
|------------|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Landscape image | 1200×628px | 1.91:1 | Yes (min 1, max 20) |
| Square image | 1200×1200px | 1:1 | Yes (min 1, max 20) |
| Portrait image | 960×1200px | 4:5 | Optional (max 20) |
| Landscape logo | 1200×300px | 4:1 | Recommended |
| Square logo | 1200×1200px | 1:1 | Yes (min 1, max 5) |

**Minimum image dimensions:**
- Landscape: 600×314px (but always design at 1200×628)
- Square: 300×300px (but always design at 1200×1200)
- Portrait: 480×600px (but always design at 960×1200)

**File requirements:**
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Max file size: 5120 KB per image
- No letter-boxing (no black bars)
- No excessive blank space

### Text Assets

| Asset Type | Max Length | Quantity |
|------------|-----------|----------|
| Short headline | 30 characters | Up to 5 |
| Long headline | 90 characters | 1 |
| Description | 90 characters | Up to 5 |
| Business name | 25 characters | 1 |
| Display URL path | 15 characters each | 2 fields |

**Text best practices:**
- Short headlines should each communicate a different value proposition
- Don't repeat the same message across headlines
- Include a CTA in at least one description
- Business name must match the advertiser identity

### Video Assets (if applicable)
- Minimum 10 seconds duration
- Horizontal (16:9), vertical (9:16), or square (1:1)
- Best practice: provide all three orientations

## Responsive Display Ads

When not using PMax, responsive display ads follow similar rules:

- Google automatically sizes and formats your assets
- Provide the best quality images at maximum dimensions
- Designs should work when cropped differently
- Keep critical content centered — edges may be trimmed
- Test how your design looks at various Google Display sizes

## Design Rules for Google Ads

### What Google Disapproves
- Misleading content or clickbait
- Excessive text that overwhelms the image
- Blurry or distorted images
- Strobing or flashing animations
- Images that mimic system UI (fake buttons, notifications)
- Sexual or shocking content

### Google's Quality Signals
- Clear, high-resolution product imagery
- Readable text at all rendered sizes
- Consistent branding across assets
- Professional, polished appearance
- Relevance between image and ad copy

### Safe Zone Considerations
- Google may overlay "Ad" badges, CTA buttons, or brand info
- Keep critical content away from corners (especially top-left and bottom-right)
- Design with 10% padding from all edges
- CTA text from Google's system overlays your image — don't duplicate
  the CTA button in your image design for PMax

### Multi-Surface Design
PMax runs across many surfaces. Design images that:
- Work on white backgrounds (Search, Shopping) AND dark (YouTube, Discover)
- Are recognizable at small sizes (Display network) AND large (YouTube)
- Communicate the message without text (text may be stripped on some surfaces)
- Have strong brand signals (logo, colors) visible even when small
